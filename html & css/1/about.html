About

Name: Vivian Ren
Born on: November 24, 1995 (Zodiac sign: Sagittarius)

Born in Tianjin, China, at Tianjin General Hospital. Arrived in Canada at the age of 4, in January of the year 2000.
Began drawing at the age of 10, influenced by Anime, mainly Inuyasha. (These drawings, however, were terrible.)
Began to draw realism a couple of years later, slowly improving with anatomy, still-life drawings, faces of characters, and everything else.

Likes: Art, Music (Rock, Techno/Electronica, C-pop/K-pop/J-pop), Anime, Video games, Reading, Ketchup chips, Sleep

Dislikes: Bitter foods, Boredom, Country music, Mondays

1 Random Fact:
Whenever she's incredibly into a game or series, she attempts to draw her characters in the style of the game/series.
